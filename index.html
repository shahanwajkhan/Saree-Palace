Support & Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your sarees authentic handlooms?

Yes, 100%. We are certified handloom partners. Each saree comes with an authenticity guarantee and descriptions of the weaver cooperative responsible for crafting it.

Do you provide blouse tailoring custom stitching services?

Yes! Every saree includes an unstitched blouse piece. If you'd like custom measurement stitching, select the tailoring option on checkout, and our stylists will contact you via WhatsApp for measurements.

What are your care instructions for silk sarees?

We highly recommend dry cleaning for all silk, crepe, and heavily embroidered sarees. Store them wrapped in pure cotton fabric or muslin sheets in a cool, dry closet. Avoid spraying perfume directly on silk fibers.

What is your return policy?

Since these are handcrafted artisanal products, we offer an easy 7-day hassle-free return window for unworn items in original packaging, provided the blouse piece is un-cut.
